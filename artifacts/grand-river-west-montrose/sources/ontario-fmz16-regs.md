# Cached: Ontario FMZ 16 Fishing Regulations Summary (official)

Source: https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary/fisheries-management-zone-16
Accessed: 2026-07-20
Updated (effective): December 08, 2025

## Zone-wide seasons and limits (verbatim extracts)
- Largemouth and Smallmouth Bass (combined): Season fourth Saturday in June to November 30; Limits S-6 and C-2.
- Northern Pike: Season January 1 to March 31 and second Saturday in May to December 31; Limits S-6 and C-2.
- Walleye and Sauger (combined): Season January 1 to March 15 and second Saturday in May to December 31; Limits S-4 and C-2; not more than 1 greater than 46 centimetres.
- Crappie: Season open all year; Limits S-30 and C-10.
- Yellow Perch: Season open all year; Limits S-50 and C-25.
- FMZ 16 is part of the Southern Bait Management Zone (BMZ).

## Grand River waterbody exceptions (verbatim)
Exception (upper Grand artificial-lure / barbless trout sections):
"Grand River - between the West Garafraxa second Line and Scotland Street in the Town of Fergus;
between Tower Street in the Town of Fergus and the Bissell Dam in Nichol Township;
between 100 metres downstream of a bridge located at the southern boundary of the Elora Gorge Conservation Area and a point 100 metres upstream of Pilkington 8th Line in Pilkington Township;
between 100 metres downstream of Pilkington 8th Line bridge and 100 metres upstream of Pilkington Township and Woolwich Township boundary line;
and between 100 metres downstream of Pilkington Township and Woolwich Township boundary line and 100 metres upstream of Highway 86 bridge"
Rules: Only artificial lures may be used; Only 1 barbless hook may be used; Brook trout, brown trout and rainbow trout - S-0 and C-0.

Exception (Grand River Paris to Brantford): artificial lures, 1 barbless hook, fish sanctuary March 1 to Friday before fourth Saturday in April; Brown trout, northern pike, rainbow trout, smallmouth bass and walleye - S-0 and C-0.
Exception (Grand River Brantford to Lake Erie): Brown trout and rainbow trout (combined) S-1 and C-0, October 1 to December 31.

## Geographic finding (regs-critical)
The upper-Grand special-regs section's downstream terminus is "100 metres upstream of Highway 86 bridge."
The Highway 86 bridge is UPSTREAM of the West Montrose covered bridge (Highway 86 bridge crosses first traveling downstream; covered bridge is downstream of it, accessed via Covered Bridge Drive heading south from Highway 86).
Therefore the covered-bridge reach (43.5866, -80.4824) lies BELOW the special section — standard FMZ 16 zone defaults apply (no artificial-lure/barbless restriction, no sanctuary). The special trout section ends just upstream near the Highway 86 bridge.
