# Lake Research: Fairy Lake (Acton)

> **Disambiguation:** This is the Fairy Lake in **Acton, Halton Hills, Ontario** (~43.6248, -80.0485), a dammed mill pond at the headwaters of Black Creek. It is NOT the Fairy Lake at Huntsville (Muskoka/Parry Sound District, a large deep coldwater lake) nor the Fairy Lake at Newmarket. Several Ontario datasets return the wrong Fairy Lake — some Parry Sound District survey documents in particular. All facts below are for the Acton waterbody only.

> **FMZ verification:** Taxonomy says FMZ 16. Confirmed — Angler's Atlas places this Fairy Lake in Zone 16, and Acton/Halton Hills lies within FMZ 16. **No taxonomy discrepancy.**

> **Waterbody type verification:** Taxonomy flagged `reservoir` as UNCONFIRMED. **CONFIRMED as a reservoir/impoundment** — created in 1830 when the Adams brothers dammed Black Creek to power a flour and saw mill; the site was wetland before impoundment, and a dam still maintains water levels today (Let's Talk Halton Hills, haltonhills.ca).

## Gotchas & disambiguation
- **INVASIVE — Round Goby:** first identified in Fairy Lake August 2024, CVC-confirmed. The Mayor's Fishing Derby ("Go for the Goby") targets goby removal/awareness. Gobies prey on eggs/young of sport fish; do NOT possess live goby or use as bait (illegal in Canada) — https://cvc.ca/conversations/small-fish-big-effect-invasive-round-goby/
- **Bait Management Zone:** FMZ 16 southern BMZ — no transport of baitfish/leeches in or out — https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary/fisheries-management-zone-16
- **Invasive vegetation:** aquatic macrophytes dominated by Eurasian Water Milfoil (plus stonewort, hornwort, pondweeds); phragmites management underway — https://www.haltonhills.ca/en/explore-and-play/fairy-lake.aspx
- **Name confusion:** at least three Ontario "Fairy Lakes" (Acton, Huntsville, Newmarket) plus a Fairy Lake in Parry Sound District — Parry Sound survey data is a DIFFERENT lake and must not be attributed here.
- **Size figures vary** across sources (~26 ha to ~69 ac / 27.2 ha) — likely differing measurement of lake vs park vs open water. Treat area as "~26–27 ha".
- Gas motors prohibited; paddle-craft / small aluminum boats only.
