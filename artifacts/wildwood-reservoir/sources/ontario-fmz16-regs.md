Effective Date: December 08, 2025

## Zone-wide species regulations (verbatim)
- Walleye and Sauger Combined — Season: January 1 to March 15 and second Saturday in May to December 31 — Limits: S-4 and C-2; not more than 1 greater than 46 centimetres
- Northern Pike — Season: January 1 to March 31 and second Saturday in May to December 31 — Limits: S-6 and C-2
- Largemouth and Smallmouth Bass Combined — Season: fourth Saturday in June to November 30 — Limits: S-6 and C-2
- Crappie — Season: open all year — Limits: S-30 and C-10
- Yellow Perch — Season: open all year — Limits: S-50 and C-25
- Sunfish — Season: open all year — Limits: S-50 and C-25

## Waterbody exceptions / sanctuaries
Wildwood Lake, Wildwood Reservoir, and Trout Creek do NOT appear in the FMZ 16 zone
exceptions or sanctuary lists — zone-wide regulations apply. (The Thames River proper and
several tributaries carry their own exceptions; the reservoir does not.)

## Southern Bait Management Zone
"Live or dead baitfish or leeches may not be transported into or out of a BMZ. These rules
do not apply to baitfish and leeches which are both dead and preserved." FMZ 16 is within
the Southern Bait Management Zone.
