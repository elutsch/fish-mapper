# Species Fit: Fanshawe Lake

## Summary
- **Destination species:** (none)
- **Strong species:** Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Walleye
- **Headline structure surfaced:** A drowned North Thames River valley behind Fanshawe Dam — the deeper, clearer basin and sand/clay/**rock** bottom toward the dam (southwest) for smallmouth; the shallow, vegetated upper (north) end and backwater bays for largemouth and pike; and the North Thames inflow plus the old river channel through the reservoir for walleye. Water is eutrophic, stained/turbid and river-fed, with a max depth near 40 ft (mean ~11.7 ft).
