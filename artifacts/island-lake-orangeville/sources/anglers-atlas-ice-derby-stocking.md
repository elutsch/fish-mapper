# Angler data, derby, stocking, boating

## Anglers Atlas — Orangeville Reservoir (angler-contributed)
URL: https://www.anglersatlas.com/place/124687/orangeville-reservoir — accessed 2026-07-19
- Surface area: 179.4 ha (~443 acres / 1.8 km2). Coordinates listed on page: 43.9388, -80.0813 (angler-platform pin — NOT used for profile; profile uses data/spots.json 43.9273, -80.0751).
- Species confirmations: Northern Pike 15, Largemouth Bass 11, Yellow Perch 10, Pumpkinseed 9, Brown Bullhead 7, Black Crappie 6, Bluegill 6, Brook Trout 2 (disputed, 6 disagreements).
- Angler reports: pike on salted smelt/quick-strike rigs; topwater; east arm noted closed seasonally in winter.

## CVC Foundation — Island Lake Ice Derby (quality signal)
URL: https://cvcfoundation.ca/island-lake-ice-derby — accessed 2026-07-19
- Annual ice fishing derby at Island Lake Conservation Area. 2026 edition: February 7–16, 2026.
- Categories: Northern Pike (adult), Black Crappie, Yellow Perch, Catfish (youth).
- 2026 adult pike winners: 1st 825 mm ($1,000), 2nd 695 mm, 3rd 655 mm. Crappie and perch category prizes $375 each.
- Valid Ontario fishing licence required (Ontario Family Fishing Weekend Feb 14–16, licence-free).

## Ontario Fish ON-Line stocking dataset (OFFICIAL)
URL: https://www.lioapplications.lrc.gov.on.ca/services/FishONLine/StockingList.aspx?json=true&fmz=16&stock=AllSpecies&diagnostics=false&lang=en — accessed 2026-07-19
- No stocking record for Island Lake / Orangeville Reservoir. Credit River stocking records are at Town of Caledon (downstream, distinct): Chinook/Coho salmon, Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout. Island Lake gamefish appear self-sustaining/naturalized.

## History (angler-reported)
iFish Ontario / forum snippets (http://www.ifishontario.com/lakeFeedback.php?id=5643, 404 on direct fetch; via search): Island Lake was a stocked trout lake ~25 years ago; Credit River overflow introduced pike/bass, plus illegal bass stocking, which displaced most trout. Corroborated loosely by Anglers Atlas "brook trout — disputed" tag. (source: angler report — user-contributed)

## CVC — Canoeing, Kayaking and Boating (OFFICIAL)
URL: https://cvc.ca/explore-activities/canoeing-kayaking-boating/ — accessed 2026-07-19
- Trailer boat launch: parking lot P1; $8 + HST (free with Credit Valley Parks Pass); concrete surface + dock.
- Free walk-in / hand launch: parking lot P1, no fee.
- "Gas powered motors are not permitted on Island Lake." Electric boat rentals discontinued.
- Rentals: canoes (up to 3 seats), single/tandem sit-on & sit-in kayaks, fishing kayaks with rod holders; lifejacket/safety kit/paddles included.
- Paddle craft storage by appointment.
