## Zone-wide species regulations (verbatim wording extracted)

**Largemouth and Smallmouth Bass (combined)**
- Season: "fourth Saturday in June to November 30"
- Limits: "S-6 and C-2"
- Size limit: none specified

**Northern Pike**
- Season: "January 1 to March 31 and second Saturday in May to December 31"
- Limits: "S-6 and C-2"
- Size limit: none specified

**Walleye and Sauger (combined)**
- Season: "January 1 to March 15 and second Saturday in May to December 31"
- Limits: "S-4 and C-2; not more than 1 greater than 46 centimetres"

**Crappie**
- Season: "open all year"
- Limits: "S-30 and C-10"

**Yellow Perch**
- Season: "open all year"
- Limits: "S-50 and C-25"

## Waterbody exceptions / sanctuaries
- No "Christie" waterbody exception or fish sanctuary appears in FMZ 16.
- Spencer Creek exception (downstream of the reservoir, NOT the lake): "Spencer Creek — City of Hamilton, from Highway 102 (Cootes Drive) to Lake Ontario" listed under additional fishing opportunities for Atlantic salmon, brown trout, Pacific salmon and rainbow trout, open all year.

## Bait
- "FMZ 16 is part of the Southern Bait Management Zone (BMZ). Live or dead baitfish or leeches may not be transported into or out of a BMZ."
