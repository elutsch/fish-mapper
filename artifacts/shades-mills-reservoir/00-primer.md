# Lake Primer

## Physical character
Reservoir on Mill Creek in Cambridge, created by the Shade's Mills Dam. Small impoundment at the centre of a day-use conservation area, minutes from the city.

## Morphology signal
Small reservoir with warm, weedy character suited to warmwater species — largemouth bass and panfish thrive here. Depth character not established in initial searches; assume modest depths typical of a small creek impoundment pending Stage 1a confirmation.

## Watershed & access
Part of the Grand River watershed via Mill Creek, managed as a Grand River Conservation Authority day-use conservation area. Single boat launch, electric motors only; on-site rentals for paddle craft, and ice fishing offered in winter.

## Managing authorities
- Grand River Conservation Authority
- Ontario Ministry of Natural Resources (FMZ 16)

## Fishery reputation signal
Reservoir known for northern pike, largemouth bass, smallmouth bass, yellow perch, and black crappie; also a local ice-fishing spot in winter.
