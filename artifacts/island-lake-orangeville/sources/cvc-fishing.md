# Credit Valley Conservation — Fishing (OFFICIAL)
URL: https://cvc.ca/explore-activities/fishing/
Accessed: 2026-07-19

Species: "A variety of fish make Island Lake their home, including largemouth bass, Northern pike, black crappie, yellow perch and other pan fish."

Lake access: 400-acre lake; boat and shore fishing; "Try one of our 10 accessible fishing piers on the south shore."

Shore fishing rules: anglers can only fish "from the shore on the south side of the lake (the same side as parking lots P1 – P4)." Fishing prohibited on bridges or from dams.

Ice fishing: "Ice fishing is only permitted at Island Lake Conservation Area" within CVC parks. Requires "at least 5 inches of ice"; restricted to accessible zones reachable from parking lot P1. Season January through March, conditions permitting.

Bait & tackle: worms by the dozen (summer); 3-4 inch emerald shiners (minnows) in winter; artificial lures year-round.

Rentals: fishing rods (TackleShare), watercraft, ice fishing rods, hole auguring, ice hut rentals (seasonal).

Events: annual Ice Fishing Derby in winter.

# CVC — Fishing Regulations (OFFICIAL)
URL: https://cvc.ca/fishing-regulations/
Accessed: 2026-07-19

- FMZ: Credit River / Island Lake falls under FMZ 16.
- Bait: outside baitfish prohibited except those "purchased at the Island Lake shop"; worms permitted where Ontario regs allow; "CVC does not allow the use of or harvesting of baitfish, frogs, leeches or crayfish in our parks."
- Bass: Largemouth bass "Catch and release" — no harvest allowed (CVC property rule).
- Boat access: fishing "during hours of operation and boat access only."
- Panfish (crappie, perch, sunfish): open all year; limits per FMZ 16.
