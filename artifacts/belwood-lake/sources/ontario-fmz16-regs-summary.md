## Zone-wide seasons and limits (verbatim)

- **Walleye and Sauger Combined** — Season: "January 1 to March 15 and second Saturday in May to December 31" — Limits: "S-4 and C-2; not more than 1 greater than 46 centimetres"
- **Northern Pike** — Season: "January 1 to March 31 and second Saturday in May to December 31" — Limits: "S-6 and C-2"
- **Largemouth and Smallmouth Bass Combined** — Season: "fourth Saturday in June to November 30" — Limits: "S-6 and C-2"
- **Yellow Perch** — Season: "open all year" — Limits: "S-50 and C-25"
- **Crappie** — Season: "open all year" — Limits: "S-30 and C-10"
- **Sunfish** — Season: "open all year" — Limits: "S-50 and C-25"

## Bass season check
The only bass season listed in the summary is "fourth Saturday in June to November 30". No zone-wide catch-and-release-only bass season before the June opener, and no extended December bass season, appears in the official summary.

## Bait Management Zone
FMZ 16 is part of the "Southern Bait Management Zone (BMZ)" — restrictions on transporting live/dead baitfish and leeches.

## Grand River exception (RIVER, not the reservoir)
Grand River near Fergus has specialized regs for trout: "Only artificial lures may be used" and "Only 1 barbless hook may be used" with S-0 and C-0 in specific sections. No Belwood Lake (reservoir) exceptions found in the document.
