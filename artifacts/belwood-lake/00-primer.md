# Lake Primer

## Physical character
Flood-control reservoir on the upper Grand River behind Shand Dam, north of Fergus. Long, narrow impoundment following the former river valley, also used for low-flow augmentation and some hydro generation.

## Morphology signal
Mid-sized reservoir with mixed depth character — shallower water in the upper river arm where the Grand enters, deeper water toward the dam. Water levels fluctuate seasonally with flood-control drawdown. Generally warm-water habitat.

## Watershed & access
Part of the upper Grand River watershed, operated by the Grand River Conservation Authority. Public boat launch at Belwood Lake Conservation Area near Fergus; access is tied to the conservation area. Coldwater tailwater below the dam is a separate river fishery, not part of this profile.
