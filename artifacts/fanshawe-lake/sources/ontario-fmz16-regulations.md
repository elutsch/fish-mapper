## Zone-wide species regulations (verbatim)

**Walleye and Sauger Combined**
- Season: "January 1 to March 15 and second Saturday in May to December 31"
- Limits: "S–4 and C–2; not more than 1 greater than 46 centimetres"

**Northern Pike**
- Season: "January 1 to March 31 and second Saturday in May to December 31"
- Limits: "S–6 and C–2"

**Largemouth and Smallmouth Bass Combined**
- Season: "fourth Saturday in June to November 30"
- Limits: "S–6 and C–2"

**Crappie**
- Season: "open all year"
- Limits: "S–30 and C–10"

**Yellow Perch**
- Season: "open all year"
- Limits: "S–50 and C–25"

**Sunfish**
- Season: "open all year"
- Limits: "S–50 and C–25"

## Waterbody / exception findings
- No waterbody exception mentions Fanshawe Lake, Fanshawe Reservoir, or the North Thames River for the six target gamefish.
- The North Thames River appears in species exceptions for brown trout and rainbow trout ("open all year"). Not applicable to the six target gamefish at Fanshawe.
- Fanshawe Reservoir takes the zone-wide FMZ 16 base regulations.
