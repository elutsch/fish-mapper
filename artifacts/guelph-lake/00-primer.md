# Lake Primer

## Physical character
Reservoir on the Speed River behind the Guelph Dam, north of Guelph. Impoundment flooding the former river valley, surrounded by the Guelph Lake Conservation Area.

## Morphology signal
Mid-sized reservoir, mixed depth — shallower weedy water in the upper river arms, deeper water toward the dam. Water is somewhat stained, as is typical of a Grand-system reservoir.

## Watershed & access
Part of the Speed River / Grand River watershed, managed as a Grand River Conservation Authority reservoir. Public boat launches at the conservation area (electric trolling motors only; outboards and personal watercraft prohibited); seasonal access tied to the park being open.

## Managing authorities
- Grand River Conservation Authority (GRCA)
- Ontario Ministry of Natural Resources (FMZ 16)

## Fishery reputation signal
Reservoir known for smallmouth and largemouth bass, northern pike, and panfish including black crappie and yellow perch.
