# Lake Primer

## Physical character
Reservoir formed by a dam on Laurel Creek within Laurel Creek Conservation Area in Waterloo. An urban-edge flood-control impoundment, distinct from Laurel Creek itself (which continues below the dam). FMZ 16 confirmed against the Ontario fishing regulations summary — matches taxonomy, no discrepancy.

## Morphology signal
Small, shallow reservoir with warm water; reports note it is drawn down annually, so water levels fluctuate substantially through the season. Character favours warm-water species; no deep cold-water habitat.

## Watershed & access
Laurel Creek is a tributary of the Grand River, so the reservoir sits in the Grand River watershed, managed by the Grand River Conservation Authority within a fee-access conservation area. Paddle craft and electric trolling motors only — no power boats; camping and day-use park access.
