## Zone-wide seasons and limits (exact wording)

- Walleye and Sauger Combined — Season: "January 1 to March 15 and second Saturday in May to December 31" — Limits: S-4 and C-2 — Size: "not more than 1 greater than 46 centimetres"
- Yellow Perch — Season: "open all year" — Limits: S-50 and C-25 — Size: none
- Crappie — Season: "open all year" — Limits: S-30 and C-10 — Size: none
- Sunfish — Season: "open all year" — Limits: S-50 and C-25 — Size: none

## Waterbody exception involving this lake (exact wording)
- "Thames River - between the dam at Gordon Pittock Reservoir and the bridge at Highway 59" — "Fish sanctuary - no fishing from March 15 to Friday before second Saturday in May"

No references to Woodstock or "Pittock Dam" (as a named exception) appear in the document; the reservoir itself carries no in-lake season/limit exception — only the downstream Thames River sanctuary reach above.
