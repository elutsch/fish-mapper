# Lake Primer

## Physical character
Man-made reservoir behind the Christie Dam on Spencer Creek, at Christie Lake Conservation Area in Dundas (Hamilton). Flood-control impoundment operated by the Hamilton Conservation Authority. Not the natural Christie Lake near Perth (Tay River, FMZ 18) — that is a different waterbody.

## Morphology signal
Small, shallow, warm-water reservoir with weedy margins — habitat suited to warm-water species, not cold-water ones. Water character [NOT FOUND].

## Watershed & access
Part of the Spencer Creek watershed draining toward Cootes Paradise and Hamilton Harbour on Lake Ontario. Access through the gated conservation area (day-use fee); shore, dock, and small-boat fishing with electric motors only, no gasoline motors; canoe and paddleboard rentals on site.

## Managing authorities
- Hamilton Conservation Authority
- Ontario Ministry of Natural Resources (FMZ 16)

## Fishery reputation signal
Family-oriented conservation-area fishery known for largemouth bass, northern pike, and panfish (crappie, bluegill, perch), plus catfish and carp. HCA promotes a voluntary catch-and-release policy to sustain the native fishery.
