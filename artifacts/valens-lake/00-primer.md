# Lake Primer

> **FLAG — taxonomy discrepancy:** `taxonomy/lakes.md` disambiguates Valens Lake as a "Conservation Halton reservoir." Location and slug are otherwise correct; downstream stages should cite HCA, not Conservation Halton. FMZ 16 is consistent with official Ontario sources for Flamborough-area waters.

## Physical character
Man-made flood-control reservoir near the headwaters of Spencer Creek in Flamborough, within Valens Lake Conservation Area. Built as part of the Spencer Creek watershed flood program.

## Morphology signal
Small reservoir with generally shallow, warm-water character suited to bass, pike, and panfish; not deep coldwater habitat.

## Watershed & access
Part of the Spencer Creek watershed, which drains off the Niagara Escarpment to Cootes Paradise on Lake Ontario. Access is through the conservation area (day-use fee); shoreline, dock, and small-craft fishing, with gasoline motors not permitted and rentals available on site.
