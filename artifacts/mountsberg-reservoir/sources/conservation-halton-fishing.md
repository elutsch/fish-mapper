## Mountsberg species listed
"Northern Pike, Small and Largemouth Bass" (Conservation Halton names smallmouth AND largemouth for Mountsberg).

## Fishing permitted
Yes — fly fishing and shoreline fishing allowed, plus non-motorized boat fishing.

## Boat / launch rules
Canoes/kayaks may be launched at Mountsberg Dam or carried from the parking lot to shore. Larger non-motorized boats launch only at the Dam. Exception: "Boats with electric trolling motors are permitted as an exception."

## Live bait
Avoid "live minnows, crawfish, and frogs." Worms permitted if purchased from proper bait suppliers or the park.

## Licence
"A valid Ontario Fishing License" required for those 18 and older.

## Conservation Halton properties allowing fishing
Kelso, Hilton Falls, Mountsberg, Robert Edmondson (plus Area 8 for special permissions/events only). Prohibited at Crawford Lake and wherever posted "No Fishing."
