## Seasonal patterns
- Best-season by species (general regional patterns): Pike — cold-water periods, early open water and through the ice; strong winter target. Largemouth — summer through fall in weeds/coves (season opens 4th Sat June). Crappie/Perch/panfish — open all year, major winter ice-fishery draw.
- Spawn windows: [NOT FOUND lake-specific]. General FMZ 16 regional patterns — pike spawn early spring (ice-out, in shallow flooded wetland margins), largemouth late spring/early summer (~water 16–18°C), crappie/panfish late spring. (general regional pattern, not lake-documented)
- Turnover / thermocline notes: [NOT FOUND] — shallow weedy reservoir; limited stratification expected but not documented.
- Ice fishing: Season roughly January–March, only when at least 5 inches (12.5 cm) of ice has formed; ice accessed only from parking lot P1 (in front of Rental Centre); restricted-marked areas closed. Ice-hut, auger and rod rentals available; winter minnows (3–4" emerald shiners) sold at shop.
