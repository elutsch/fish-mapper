# Cached: GRCA — Shade's Mills Conservation Area
URL: https://www.grandriver.ca/outdoor-recreation/conservation-areas/shades-mills/
Fetched: 2026-07-18

Species: "Northern pike, largemouth bass, smallmouth bass, yellow perch and black crappie are plentiful in the reservoir."
Size: 32-hectare (80-acre) reservoir.
Motor rule: "Only non-motorized boats and boats with electric motors are allowed."
Rentals: Serene Watersports — canoe, kayak, paddle board, pedal boat rentals.
Ice fishing: "Fishing is available on the reservoir when ice conditions are suitable. Temporary ice huts are permitted and must be removed once fishing is completed for the day."
Address: 450 Avenue Road, Cambridge, ON N1R 5S4
Season: Open all year (day use only).
