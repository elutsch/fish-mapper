# Lake Research: Valens Lake

> **FLAG — taxonomy discrepancy (carried from Stage 0 primer, confirmed here):** `taxonomy/lakes.md` disambiguates Valens Lake as a *"Conservation Halton reservoir."* This is **incorrect**. Every official HCA page and the provincial listing confirm HCA, not Conservation Halton. Slug, province, location and FMZ are otherwise correct. **FMZ 16 is verified correct** against the official Ontario Fishing Regulations Summary for this Flamborough/Spencer Creek water — no zone change needed. Downstream stages should cite HCA as the managing authority.

### Largemouth Bass

- Submerged dead-tree stumps below the surface — wood cover holding largemouth; hard to see in the dark water — https://kayakingthegta.wordpress.com/2021/10/12/valens-lake-flamborough/

### Northern Pike
- Dam-area deeper water (avg ~5–7 ft, the lake's deepest zone) — open pockets among weeds; ~50/50 pike/bass water, worked with jerkbaits (Rapala Husky Jerk/X-Rap), spinners (Vibrax), and spinnerbaits — https://www.canadian-sportfishing.com/question/hi-italo-i-will-be-fishing-valens-lake-conservation-area...
- Weed edges and weedy bays throughout — ambush habitat with abundant panfish forage; pike is the most-logged species on the lake — https://fishbrain.com/fishing-waters/meQGQMKu/valens-reservoir

### Walleye

## Seasonal patterns
- Best-season / access reality: usable open-water season effectively **begins the fourth Saturday in June**, because the whole lake is a sanctuary (closed) April 1–4th Sat June — the closure ends exactly as the bass season opens. — https://conservationhamilton.ca/activities/fishing/
- Spawn windows: the April–late-June closure is timed to protect spring spawning (pike early spring; bass/panfish late spring–early summer) — closure dates documented; exact per-species spawn dates not individually published for this lake. — https://conservationhamilton.ca/conservation-areas/valens-lake/
- Largemouth: shallow weed/stump cover (bridge basin) productive through the warm open-water months on weedless presentations. — https://www.canadian-sportfishing.com/question/hi-italo-i-will-be-fishing-valens-lake-conservation-area...
- Pike: deeper dam-area water and weed edges; hardwater season present. — https://www.canadian-sportfishing.com/question/hi-italo-i-will-be-fishing-valens-lake-conservation-area...
- Ice fishing: permitted at Valens when ice conditions allow; HCA provides no huts/equipment/pre-drilled holes and posts ice status on the Conservation Area page. Pike (Jan 1–Mar 31), crappie and perch (open all year) are the legal winter targets. — https://conservationhamilton.ca/activities/fishing/
- Turnover / thermocline: not documented; lake is shallow and unlikely to hold a strong thermocline.

## Gotchas & disambiguation
- **Managing-authority error in taxonomy:** it is Hamilton Conservation Authority, NOT Conservation Halton (see top FLAG). — https://conservationhamilton.ca/conservation-areas/valens-lake/
- **Whole-lake closure Apr 1–4th Sat June** — no fishing at all during this window; easy to miss. — https://conservationhamilton.ca/activities/fishing/
- **No gas motors** — electric trolling motors only. — https://conservationhamilton.ca/activities/fishing/
- **Navigation hazard:** submerged dead-tree stumps sit just below the surface and are near-invisible in the dark water. — https://kayakingthegta.wordpress.com/2021/10/12/valens-lake-flamborough/
- Name/geography note: Fishbrain and some sources tag it "Valens Reservoir / near Cambridge"; it is the Flamborough (City of Hamilton) reservoir — same water. — https://fishbrain.com/fishing-waters/meQGQMKu/valens-reservoir
- Southern Bait Management Zone (FMZ 16): live/dead baitfish and leeches may not be transported into/out of the BMZ (dead-and-preserved exempt) — general zone rule, verify current text. — https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary/fisheries-management-zone-16
