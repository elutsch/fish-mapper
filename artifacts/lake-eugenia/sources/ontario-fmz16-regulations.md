## Verified target-species regulations (exact wording)

- **Largemouth and Smallmouth Bass (Combined)** — Season: "fourth Saturday in June to November 30" — Limits: "S-6 and C-2" — Size limit: none specified.
- **Yellow Perch** — Season: "open all year" — Limits: "S-50 and C-25" — Size limit: none specified.
- **Rainbow Trout** (secondary/non-taxonomy interest) — Season: "fourth Saturday in April to September 30" — Limits: "S-2 and C-1" — Size limit: none specified. (Resolves the 1a conflicting extraction S-5/C-2 vs S-2/C-1 → official is S-2/C-1.)

## Waterbody exceptions
- No exception listed for "Eugenia," "Lake Eugenia," or the Grey County Beaver River above the dam under FMZ 16 waterbody exceptions. Zone-wide FMZ 16 rules apply to the lake.
- The "Beaver River — Collingwood Township, Thornbury Dam to Georgian Bay (Grey County)" entry (Additional fishing opportunities: Atlantic salmon, brown trout, Pacific salmon, rainbow trout; open fourth Saturday in April to December 31) is the DOWNSTREAM river below the dam — a different regulatory regime; it does NOT apply to Lake Eugenia.

## Bait
- FMZ 16 sits within the Southern Bait Management Zone (transport restrictions on live/dead baitfish and leeches). (Per FMZ 16 summary; consistent with the Conestogo reference.)
