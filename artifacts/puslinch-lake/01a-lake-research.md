## In-lake structure (by species)
Named-structure inventory is genuinely thin for this lake: it is a shallow weed bowl with "few shoals and one main deep area" (thefishinguide). Only species with sourced structure or documented seasonal pattern are listed.

### Largemouth Bass
- Lake-wide shallow weed flats (coontail, milfoil, cabbage; most of lake <2 m) — bass "literally everywhere in the lake," caught on spinnerbaits and stick baits; early morning topwater best — https://www.thefishinguide.com/puslinch_lake_ON.php ; weed types: https://adventurefishing.ca/...
- Weed edges in 6–8 ft of water adjacent to the deep lake basin — fall (October) fish stack here as water cools; spinnerbaits and square-bill crankbaits — (source: angler report — user-contributed) https://argosgirloutdoors.com/2011/10/12/thanksgiving-fishing-at-puslinch-lake/
- Lily-pad bays and rocky shoreline points — frog/topwater water in warm months — (source: angler report — user-contributed) https://argosgirloutdoors.com/2011/10/12/thanksgiving-fishing-at-puslinch-lake/

### Northern Pike
- Shallow weedbeds, especially in spring — "best in the shallows, especially in the spring. Spinnerbaits in dark colours can be very effective" — https://www.thefishinguide.com/puslinch_lake_ON.php
- Weed edge at 6–8 ft beside the deep basin — fall-transition pike caught alongside largemouth — (source: angler report — user-contributed) https://argosgirloutdoors.com/2011/10/12/thanksgiving-fishing-at-puslinch-lake/
- Through the ice, lake-wide shallow weed cover — "excellent shallow lake that has a ton of pike, mostly 2–5 lbs but there are some reaching about 15 lbs" — (source: angler report — user-contributed, forum snippet) https://www.ontariofishingforums.com/threads/puslinch-lake-ice-fishing.12999/ [page fetch blocked HTTP 403; claim from search result excerpt]

### Walleye
- The single main deep hole off McCormick's Point (lake max ~5.5 m; deepest area is only ~0.4% of the lake) — the lake's one deep refuge; "few shoals and one main deep area located off McCormick's Point" — https://www.thefishinguide.com/puslinch_lake_ON.php ; deep-area share: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puslinch_Lake
- Harvey's Island — walleye ("pickerel") spawning stones placed near the island in 1998 by the PLCA to promote natural reproduction; the lake's named spring-spawn structure — https://www.myplca.com/lake-restoration
- Evening bite on bottom-fished worms or trolled worm harnesses; average fish ~1 lb — https://www.thefishinguide.com/puslinch_lake_ON.php

### Black Crappie
- Spring shallows — "most commonly caught in the spring, fishing small jigs under floats"; also taken trolling small spinners tipped with worms (with bonus walleye) — https://www.thefishinguide.com/puslinch_lake_ON.php

### Yellow Perch
- No named structure found; documented as a top-three catch (Fishbrain logs) and a winter/ice target in angler discussion — https://fishbrain.com/fishing-waters/54vh8wPa/puslinch-lake ; (forum snippet, fetch blocked) https://www.lakesimcoeoutdoors.com/forums/index.php?showtopic=5573

### Smallmouth Bass
- No structure entry — population "considered rare" after failed 1946–1960 stockings; do not pad — https://www.thefishinguide.com/puslinch_lake_ON.php ; MNRF Historical Fish Stocking service
