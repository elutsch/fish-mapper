## Wikipedia Mill Creek, Puslinch — https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mill_Creek,_Puslinch
Groundwater-fed stream, Puslinch. Originally brook trout; brown trout introduced 1940/1950; province stocks brown trout annually. 104 km2 subwatershed. Outlets to Grand River in downtown Cambridge after Shade's Mills Reservoir and Dam.
