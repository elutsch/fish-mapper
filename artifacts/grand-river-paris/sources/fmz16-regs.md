# CACHE — FMZ 16 Fishing Regulations Summary (Government of Ontario)

URL: https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary/fisheries-management-zone-16
Accessed: 2026-07-20
Effective date of summary: 2025-12-08

## Base zone regulations (FMZ 16)
- Smallmouth & Largemouth Bass (combined): Season fourth Saturday in June to November 30; S-6, C-2; no size limit.
- Walleye & Sauger (combined): Season Jan 1 to Mar 15 and second Saturday in May to Dec 31; S-4, C-2; not more than 1 greater than 46 cm.
- Northern Pike: Season Jan 1 to Mar 31 and second Saturday in May to Dec 31; S-6, C-2; no size limit.
- Black Crappie: Open all year; S-30, C-10.
- Yellow Perch: Open all year; S-50, C-25.

## Grand River — Paris section exception (Exceptional Waters)
"Only artificial lures may be used. Only 1 barbless hook may be used." Brown trout, northern pike, rainbow trout, smallmouth bass and walleye — S-0 and C-0 (catch-and-release only).
Boundary: from a line across the Grand River in Paris at 100 m downstream of the Hwy 2 bridge, downstream to the pedestrian and service bridge that crosses the Grand River on an angle upstream (west) of the Brant Conservation Area in the City of Brantford.

## Grand River sanctuaries (no fishing)
- October 1 to November 30: Penman Dam to William Street Bridge, Paris.
- March 1 to the Friday before the fourth Saturday in April: Paris to Brantford pedestrian bridge.

## Bait Management Zone
FMZ 16 is within the Southern Bait Management Zone. Live or dead baitfish and leeches may not be transported into or out of a BMZ (except baitfish/leeches that are both dead and preserved). Note: the Paris special-reg section is artificial-lure-only regardless.
