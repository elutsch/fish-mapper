# Lake Primer

## Physical character
Artificial reservoir on upper Spencer Creek in Flamborough, created by the Valens Dam. The reservoir supports recreation, fish habitat, summer flow augmentation, and limited flood control.

## Morphology signal
Small, shallow warmwater reservoir with marshy shorelines, weed growth, and deeper open water toward the dam. It favours largemouth bass, northern pike, and panfish rather than coldwater species.

## Watershed & access
Part of the Spencer Creek watershed and managed inside Valens Lake Conservation Area. HCA provides a Boat House launch, docks, shoreline access, and rentals; gasoline motors are prohibited.

## Managing authorities
- Hamilton Conservation Authority
- Ontario Ministry of Natural Resources (FMZ 16)

## Fishery reputation signal
Local warmwater fishery built around largemouth bass, northern pike, black crappie, yellow perch, and sunfish. HCA promotes voluntary catch-and-release and closes fishing through the spring spawning period.
