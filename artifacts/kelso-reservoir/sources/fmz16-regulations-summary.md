## Zone-wide species regulations (verbatim extract)

- **Largemouth and Smallmouth Bass (combined)** — Season: fourth Saturday in June to November 30 — Limits: S-6 and C-2 — no size limit specified
- **Yellow Perch** — Season: open all year — Limits: S-50 and C-25 — no size limit specified
- **Crappie** — Season: open all year — Limits: S-30 and C-10 — no size limit specified
- **Sunfish** — Season: open all year — Limits: S-50 and C-25 — no size limit specified
- **Walleye** — Season: January 1 to March 15 and second Saturday in May to December 31 — Limits: S-4 and C-2; not more than 1 greater than 46 centimetres
- **Northern Pike** — Season: January 1 to March 31 and second Saturday in May to December 31 — Limits: S-6 and C-2 — no size limit specified
- **Brown Trout** — Season: fourth Saturday in April to September 30 — Limits: S-5 and C-2 (aggregate with other trout/salmon)
- **Rainbow Trout** — Season: fourth Saturday in April to September 30 — Limits: S-2 and C-1 (aggregate with other trout/salmon)

## Waterbody exceptions relevant to Kelso / Sixteen Mile Creek / Milton

- The document does **not** mention "Kelso" anywhere.
- **Sixteen Mile Creek (Oakville Creek)** — Town of Oakville, from Highway 2 (Lakeshore Road) to Lake Ontario — extended season: open fourth Saturday in April to December 31.
- **Sixteen Mile Creek** — Town of Lincoln, between the Queen Elizabeth Way and Lake Ontario — extended season: open fourth Saturday in April to December 31.
  (Both allow Atlantic salmon, brown trout, Pacific salmon, rainbow trout with zone-wide limits.)
- **Bronte Creek** sanctuary references the Town of Milton (Limestone Creek upstream to Steeles Avenue) — a Jan–Oct/Dec closure that does NOT pertain to Kelso Reservoir or the Milton reach of Sixteen Mile Creek.

Conclusion: No Kelso Reservoir waterbody exception, sanctuary, or closure exists. Zone-wide FMZ 16 regs apply to Kelso Reservoir. The Milton reach of Sixteen Mile Creek (which feeds/drains the reservoir) is NOT covered by the Sixteen Mile Creek exceptions (those are Oakville and Lincoln reaches only).
