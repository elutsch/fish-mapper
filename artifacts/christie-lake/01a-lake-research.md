## Gotchas & disambiguation
- **Name collision (severe):** Christie Lake near Perth, ON (Tay River, FMZ 18) dominates search results — Fishbrain's "christie-lake" water page, the Fish-Hawk forum thread (islands, walleye, marina), onX Fish page, RVCA water-quality reports ("Good to Very Good 2006–2017"), and Christie Lake Association water-level pages ALL refer to the Perth lake. None of that applies here. The correct Fishbrain page for this waterbody is "Christie Reservoir" (b5XVsYTt).
- **Sanctuary status mismatch:** HCA's site calls Christie Lake a "fish sanctuary" closed Apr 1 to the fourth Saturday in June, but the official FMZ 16 regulations list no Christie sanctuary or waterbody exception. Treat the closure as an enforceable landowner/property rule (gated park), and do not present it as a provincial sanctuary — https://conservationhamilton.ca/activities/fishing/ vs https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary/fisheries-management-zone-16
- **Trout-stocking myth:** third-party pages (TripAdvisor snippets, blogs) claim rainbow trout stocking; the official Ontario stocking dataset shows zero records for this reservoir 2016–2026. Do not carry trout into any downstream copy.
- Gasoline motors prohibited; electric trolling motors only — https://conservationhamilton.ca/conservation-areas/christie-lake/
