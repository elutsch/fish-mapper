## Zone-wide seasons and limits (FMZ 16)
- Smallmouth & Largemouth Bass (combined): fourth Saturday in June to November 30 — S-6 / C-2
- Northern Pike: January 1 to March 31 and second Saturday in May to December 31 — S-6 / C-2 (no size limit)
- Walleye and Sauger (combined): January 1 to March 15 and second Saturday in May to December 31 — S-4 / C-2, not more than 1 greater than 46 cm
- Black Crappie: open all year — S-30 / C-10
- Yellow Perch: open all year — S-50 / C-25

## Grand River waterbody exceptions (verbatim points)
### Grand River — Paris to Brantford
Boundary: "from a line across the Grand River in Paris at 100 metres downstream of the Highway 2 (Dundas Street West) bridge downstream to the pedestrian and service bridge" in Brantford.
- Only artificial lures may be used
- Only 1 barbless hook may be used
- Fish sanctuary — no fishing from March 1 to Friday before fourth Saturday in April
- Brown trout, northern pike, rainbow trout, smallmouth bass and walleye — S-0 and C-0

### Grand River — Brantford to Lake Erie
Boundary: "from the pedestrian and service bridge ... to the edge of Lake Erie"
- Brown trout and rainbow trout (combined) — S-1 and C-0; from October 1 to December 31

## Bait
FMZ 16 is within the Southern Bait Management Zone (baitfish/leech transport restrictions apply).
