# Valens Lake source cache notes

Fetched and verified 2026-07-19. These are short audit notes, not replacements for the linked source pages.

## HCA Valens Lake Master Plan

Source: https://conservationhamilton.ca/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/Final-Approved-Valens-Master-Plan-2019.pdf

- Section 4.5: reservoir surface area 76 ha at full capacity; maximum depth 4.6 m.
- Section 5.3: artificial reservoir approximately 1.6 km long; maximum depth is at the dam; recreation level maintained near 275 m.
- Section 5.8: HCA says 14 fish species live in the lake; Largemouth Bass catch remained relatively stable over the electrofishing sampling period; Yellow Perch arrived between 2010 and 2012.
- Table 9 identifies lake records for Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Black Crappie, Yellow Perch, Bluegill, Pumpkinseed, several forage species, bullheads, Common Carp, and White Sucker.
- Property history: dam completed 1966, day-use opening June 28, 1968, 140 m fishing bridge completed 1977.

## HCA current fishing and boating pages

Sources:
- https://conservationhamilton.ca/activities/fishing/
- https://conservationhamilton.ca/conservation-areas/valens-lake/
- https://conservationhamilton.ca/activities/boating/
- https://conservationhamilton.ca/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/BoDAgendapkg_26June4.pdf

- Fishing closed April 1 to the fourth Saturday in June annually; 2026 fishing opened June 27.
- Current target list includes largemouth bass, northern pike, crappie, perch, and other panfish.
- Electric trolling motors and non-powered craft only; gasoline motors prohibited.
- Boat House launch and seasonal canoe/kayak/rowboat/SUP rentals.

## Ontario FMZ 16

Source: https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary/fisheries-management-zone-16

- Page updated 2025-12-08 for the 2026 summary.
- Bass combined: fourth Saturday in June to November 30; S-6, C-2.
- Northern Pike: January 1 to March 31 and second Saturday in May to December 31; S-6, C-2.
- Crappie: open all year; S-30, C-10.
- Yellow Perch: open all year; S-50, C-25.
- Valens Lake is not named in the page's waterbody exceptions or fish-sanctuary list; the HCA closure is therefore represented separately as an HCA property/access rule.
